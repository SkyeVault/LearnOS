## Product safety rule

Learning World does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. The movement section tells learners to stop for pain, dizziness, or feeling unwell and directs families with health concerns, injuries, pregnancy, or balance needs to a qualified health professional and appropriately trained instructor.
